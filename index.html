About my work

I am dedicated to creating visually appealing user interfaces that prioritize comfort and accessibility in user experience. While I am continually learning and refining my skills, I am committed to mastering the art of combining aesthetics with functionality.

MochiMochi Apparel

This is a website designed for an apparel shop. MochiMochi is a business that sells apparels with the goal to provide comfort and bring a wide variety of designs that can be worn by all genders. I was in charge of designing the websites page for MochiMochi Apparel. We creted a website that could help the business owners sell their apparels online. Also, it helps the monitor the number of stocks that they have in the inventory

EZ AR Booking

This is a wireframe for a site that aims to help apartment renters market or spread word about their apartments that are available for rent. It also helps people look for apartments available for rent much faster and easier. I was in charge of the layout design of this website

Protocol Ronin

This game was a project done in pairs for my Game Development subject. We have been planning to finish it so the project is still under development. It is a story of a robot gone rogue and on the hunt of taking down the head of a major corporation to bring an end towards corruption. Aside from the helping with the game's design, I was in charge of the character design and its assets.

Siomai ni Minggay

Siomai ni Minggay is centered around a cat named Minggay whose inherited a siomai restaurant from her owner. Minggay must run the restaurant and make her owner proud. This was a group project where I was assigned to the game's prototyping and wireframing.

A-List Realty

A website made for my group's capstone project. A-List Realty is a listing and blogging web system that helps showcase real estate properties in the Philippines. I was mostly doing the UI part of the project and the functionalities to implement were split among groupmates.
